Dexter is a <a href="https://www.akc.org/expert-advice/dog-breeds/soft-coated-wheaten-terrier-history-ireland/">Soft Coated Wheaten Terrier</a>. 
He loves going to the beach, where he gets to run and chase seagulls.

Dexter earned a Dogtorate in Economics. His dissertation, titled <a href="https://www.dropbox.com/scl/fi/0e5j0ucjibjqztcv8opdc/dex_dissertation.pdf?rlkey=7veh7w5jlq5h0qv9qo68bjae8&dl=0">Essays on Treat Demand and Cuddle Supply</a>, combines theory and experiments to estimate human willingness to pay for cuddles.
