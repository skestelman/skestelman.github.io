Diversity in Economics

I believe that greater diversity in Economics will lead to better science in our field. We often don't know what we don't know, so if everyone in our departments looks the same, our research will not the full set of possible questions. I am very invested in increasing diversity in economics by increasing retention of diverse aspiring economists at the beginning of the pipeline. This section of my website links to resources and papers I have come across through my involvement with initiatives to increase diversity in Economics.
